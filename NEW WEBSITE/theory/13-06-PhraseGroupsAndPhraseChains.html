Section 13.6 Phrase Groups and Phrase Chains

Phrase groups and phrase chains are not periods. In phrase groups and phrase chains, the final cadence is a half cadence. A phrase group consists of at least two phrases whose melodies begin similarly and has the final phrase ending in a half cadence.
